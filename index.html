OUR FACILITY

Our facility includes 5 acres farm and a herb of cows. The cows are fed with high qualited food , good shed and we als ensure that dairy cows are comfortable and managed in an environment to improve their health and prevent diseases.

OUR PRODUCTS

Our products include milk, ghee, cheese, butter, fresh and paneer. Our milk is instantly pasteurized ,chilled and packed in bottles . Milk being our main product is delivered fresh and chilled to 4 degree Celsius to your doorstep.

WHO WE ARE

We started in 2013 as a small unit and now its well-established. In the beginning we had very few cows and were supplying to the nearby areas, as the demand for our milk was high we started rearing ample of cows. We supply 250 litres of milk to the market on a daily basis.

Welcome to Milky Dairy Farm

Whichever dairy product is your favourite, you can be sure that it comes from a farmer-owned dairy company. Nine out of 10 litres of Amrutha milk comes from the farmers who own Amrutha and it is carefully handled by the dedicated team at Amrutha which is passionate about bringing milk's natural goodness to you and your family.
